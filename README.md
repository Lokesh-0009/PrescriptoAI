# AI Health Risk and Safety Analyzer

## Overview

AI Health Risk and Safety Analyzer is an intelligent healthcare application designed to assist users in understanding potential health risks through Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The platform analyzes user-provided symptoms and health-related information to generate risk assessments, disease predictions, personalized health insights, and treatment recommendations.

The primary objective of this project is to improve health awareness, support early risk detection, and provide accessible healthcare guidance through an interactive AI-powered system.

---

## Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle to identify potential health risks at an early stage due to limited medical knowledge and lack of immediate access to healthcare professionals. Delayed diagnosis can lead to serious health complications.

The AI Health Risk and Safety Analyzer addresses this challenge by providing a smart healthcare assistant that helps users evaluate symptoms, understand possible health conditions, and receive preliminary recommendations.
